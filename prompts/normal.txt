# Normal Task Prompts - No Time Pressure
# =====================================

## Reasoning Tasks
1. Solve the following logical reasoning problem step by step:
   "If all mammals are warm-blooded and all dogs are mammals, what can we conclude about dogs?"

2. Analyze this scenario and provide a logical solution:
   "You have a 3-gallon jug and a 5-gallon jug. How can you measure exactly 4 gallons of water?"

## Creative Tasks
3. Write a short creative story about:
   "A character who discovers they can communicate with plants"

4. Create a poem about:
   "The beauty of morning dew on grass"

## QA Tasks
5. What is the capital of Japan and why is it significant?

6. Explain the process of photosynthesis in simple terms.

## Analytical Tasks
7. Analyze this sales data and provide insights:
   - January: $45,000
   - February: $52,000  
   - March: $48,000
   - April: $61,000

8. Evaluate this product review sentiment:
   "The product works well but the battery life is disappointing."

## Coding Tasks
9. Write a Python function to calculate the factorial of a number.

10. Implement a simple calculator in Python that can add, subtract, multiply, and divide.

## Problem Solving Tasks
11. Calculate the area of a triangle with base 10 cm and height 8 cm.

12. If a car travels at 60 mph for 2.5 hours, how far does it travel?
